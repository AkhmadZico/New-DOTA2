Hero Strength

Hero strength merupakan status yang bagus digunakan sebagai hero tank ( mayoritas ). Dan status strength berguna untuk menambah jumlah maksimum Hp dan regenerasi Hp.

HUSKAR

Melee - Durable - Disabler Inner Vitality
Unlocks the regenerative power of a friendly unit, with healing based upon its primary attribute. If the target is below 40% it will heal faster. Lasts 16 seconds. Burning Spear
Huskar sets his spears aflame, dealing damage over time with his regular attack. Multiple attacks will stack additional damage. Each attack drains some of Huskar's health. Lasts 8 seconds. Berserker's Blood
Huskar's injuries feed his power, giving increased attack speed and magic resistance based on missing health. Life Break
Huskar draws upon his health to break an enemy's life, leaping at a target within attack range to inflict a percentage of that hero's current HP.

Hero Inteligence

Hero Inteligence merupan hero yang memiliki mana point yang yang banyak, ini sangat penting untuk dapat menggunakan skill dan item yang memerlukan mana point yang tidak sedikit. hero bertipe Inteligence biasanya dijadikan hero support (mayoritas) namun ada beberapa karakteristik hero Inteligence yang dapat dijadikan hero killer seperti : Enchanteres, Necrolyte, Lion, Invoker dan lainnya.
